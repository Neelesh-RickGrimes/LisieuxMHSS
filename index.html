Our Mission

We, at LISIEUX SCHOOL, strive to reach out to the stars of excellence not only in educational undertaking but also in every aspect of human struggle to achieve perfection.

Our Founder

Chavarayachan as he is popularly known was born at kainakary in Kerala. He was a great visionary and a saintly priest who ventured into many humanitarian actives in his lifetime. He is the forerunner in the field of education in Kerala and he is the founder of CMI congregation, the first indigenous religious congregation in India.
