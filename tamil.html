31 OCTOBER 2020

TAMIL ELOCUTION

Lisieux Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Saibaba colony conducted Tamil Elocution Competition as part of their Ayyan Valluvar Tamil Literary Forum ( motto:Udal mannukku; Uyir Thamizhukku). To enhance students’ skills in a clear and expressive speech in Tamil with distinct pronunciation and articulation various topics were given to various categories. Students exhibited their love for language with extraordinary involvement. Amazon shopping coupons were shared as Prizes by the Principal Rev. Fr. Dr. Paul Thekkiniyath CMI in a virtual meeting. A topper was selected and awarded in the school campus.
