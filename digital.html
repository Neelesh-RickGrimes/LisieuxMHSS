THE "DIGITAL" KEY TO LOCKDOWN

While the Covid-19 virus was successful in locking all of us down inside our houses, little did it knew that the intellectual capability of human race can unlock this using the "digital" key!

As rightly stated by our honorable Prime Minister Mr. Narendra Modi, the "DIGITAL INDIA" mevement has seen its growth and reach all through our Nation during this lockdown.

Yes, we could see many examples and applications of the digital India in our day to day lives right from shopping through Amazon, bill payments, school fees remittance and entertainment through Amazon Prime, Netflix and so on.

At my house, my parents continue their job through "work from home" by connecting to their teams, customers using Microsoft products — Skype for business and MS Teams.

I and my sister Raghini (studying in Avila Convent) attend school from home! Yes, online classes using Google Meet and Google classroom brought in a feeling of surprise and enthusiasm to see our teachers and classmates live through the video which added to our joy of using the digital gadgets — smartphones, tablets, laptops...

My dad got me a new Lenove Legion laptop when my school Lisieux announced online classes! This not only opened up using digital gadgets but also created an interest in exploring the various possibilities including pragramming in the laptop!

I was always interested in electronic components — Diodes, Transistors, resistors and capacitors connected in my breadboard when | tried the alarm signals with sound and light using LEDs.

With the lockdown and absence of school hours, I studied the basics of animations and tried a few samples using the blender software in my Legion laptop! I further explored programming using Python, Ruby and was immensely happy when I did the basic programming and connected to MySQL database using Python!

I feel the digital world came as a blessing to overcome the most difficull times and also helped people maintain social distancing and meet most of their néeds being at home!

As rightly said, the cloud and internet has brought the world inte our little hands!

A long way to go further and explore the length and breadth of the digital world!!!!

"Let your ability be in piety regulating your life and controlling your senses"

Fr. Dr. Paul Thekkiniyath CMI,Principal
