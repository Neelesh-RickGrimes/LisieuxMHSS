ADMISSIONS


Admission to all classes is given purely on merit. Our institution is open to all students irrespective of caste and creed. They are accepted and cherished as they are and are encouraged to grow in their cultural, social and religious traditions.

General Information


The School Year


The school year is from June to April. The summer holidays will be from the third week of April to the last week of May.


The School Hours


The school will function from 8.30 am to 3.40 pm with 30 minutes break for Lunch from 11.55 am to 12.25 pm.

CODE OF CONDUCT
Every pupil must have a copy of the school hand book with him and should bring it to the school everyday.
The School uniform is compulsory on all school days. The pupils must come to the school neat and tidy.
The warning bell before the class is the signal for all to goto the assembley or their respective class rooms. This should be done in silence. As soon sa theyn are in class, pupils should get ready for each period.
Pupils who have been absent or who come late will be admitted in the class only with the permission of the principal.If a pupil is absent for more than five consecutive days without the permission of the principal, his name will be struck off from the rolls.
Irregular attendance, frequent failures in submitting class execises , habitual want of discipline or any sort of serious misbehaviours are sfficient reasons for the dismissal of the student.
Every pupil must behave respectfully toward the members of the teaching and non teaching staff.
Student should have respect for school property. They should not damage school furniture, write or draw on the walls, scratch or spoil desks or chairs . Any damage of school property will have to be fined for.
